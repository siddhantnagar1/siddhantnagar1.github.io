Our Mission

We strive to provide free, first-class educational and support services for cancer patients through our personalized support groups and our educational seminars hosted by the nation’s leading oncologists. We strongly believe that as a patient, you deserve to have the information you need to make the right decision for you, and OncoSupport is here to make that happen. Fill our our intake form to instantly connect with your personalized support group.

Nobody should endure cancer alone

We understand that cancer can be an isolating and overwhelming experience, often accompanied by tremendous fear and uncertainty. Unfortunately, the modern healthcare system has often overlooked or even neglected mental health support for cancer patients.

Enter OncoSupport.

OncoSupport is a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated towards providing mental health support for cancer patients. We do this by generating peer support groups of 10-20 patients and caregivers. Using your diagnosis information, including your cancer diagnosis, diagnosis stage, age, gender, location, etc., you are matched with your personal support group within seconds. With a team, you can beat cancer.

We are a Community

Here at OncoSupport, we know the power of having a supportive family during your cancer journey. Thus, we seek to create a safe and judgment-free environment for patients, families, caregivers, and healthcare workers to offer and inquire guidance for their diagnosis.

With this in mind, our team at OncoSupport is committed to the following:

Security

All information used to create support groups is given voluntarily, with no one at OncoSupport being able to access to any personal information.

Inclusivity

OncoSupport is committed to supporting cancer patients of all backgrounds. Our software specializes in connecting you with individuals similar to your background, so you never feel alone.

Education

All information used to create support groups is given voluntarily, with no one at OncoSupport being able to access to any personal information.

Involvement

OncoSupport was founded on the principles of always doing what’s in the patient’s best interest. Thus, our team is committed to stay in communication with you to see how we can better serve you.

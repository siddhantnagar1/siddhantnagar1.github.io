How do I fill out the Intake Form?

Filling out our intake form is incredibly simple. The form asks you for 12 items:

  • Full Name
  • Relationship to Patient (Patient, Parent of Patient, Caregiver, Physician/Nurse)
  • Gender
  • Age
  • Diagnosis By Location (see https://www.cancer.gov/types/by-body-location)
  • Cancer Diagnosis (see https://www.cancer.gov/types)
  • Cancer Stage (0, I, II, III, IV)
  • Time with Cancer (months)
  • Primary Speaking Language
  • Parenting Situation (Have children, planning on having children, no children)
  • Strongest Personal Concern (Financial, Emotional, Lack of Knowledge, Other)
What happens after I complete the form?

Once you have filled out our intake form, our algorithm will process your information and will redirect you to a page showing 3 things:

  • The names, ages, relationships, genders, diagnoses, and contact info of everyone in your support group
  • A Google Meet for when your support group intends to meet virtually
  • A link to your custom online support group chat

These features will allow you to (1) find out if there’s any particular individual in your support group you are interested in talking to/learning from, (2) meet virtually with your support group to offer support, give/receive advice, or to simply talk to a friend, and (3) directly call or message anyone in your support group.

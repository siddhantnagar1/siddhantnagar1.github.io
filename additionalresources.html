Connect with your multiple communities using our additional resources

Expand your community beyond your support group

Using the additional resources offered by OncoSupport, you are able to communicate with a particular community that may not be in your support group. Specifically, Onco Support provides access to 3 additional peer support groups that can be joined by any individual who belongs to that group. At OncoSupport, we firmly believe that peer support is crucial for your mental health as a cancer patient, especially being able to share your experiences and ask your questions with patients going through a similar/same journey.

The same general rules from our support group chats apply here. The meeting times are generally set within the group chats, and you are able to set up multiple meetings if everyone is not able to attend one. Below is a list of the three additional support resources offered by OncoSupport. Please note that this list is subject to change, and is likely to be extended in the near future.

Aging and Cancer treatment

Connect with a population that understands how to go about the later stages of life while being a cancer patient.

Group Chat
Video Chat

Parenting with Cancer

Support fellow mothers who understand the struggle of raising a child while being a patient.

Group Chat
Video Chat

Cancer as an Young Adult

Learn how your fellow adolescents are coping with their cancer while growing their lives.

Group Chat
Video Chat
